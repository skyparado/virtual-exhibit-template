CSARCH2 — Group 7 [S01] — 3rd Term, A.Y. 2025–2026

Full Capacity: The Evolution of Computer Data Storage is a virtual exhibit created in partial fulfillment of the course requirements for Introduction to Computer Organization and Architecture 2 (CSARCH2) at De La Salle University – Manila.

The exhibit traces the history of data storage from punch cards and magnetic drums to solid-state drives and emerging technologies like DNA storage, highlighting the architectural principles and trade-offs behind each innovation.
